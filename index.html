EDITING COACHING

We offer you writing guidance, editing, and proofreading, and we're not done until you are completely satisfied. We coach you personally to discuss your writing and our editing. Here's how the process works:

  • You send us your document. You tell us your time constraints.
  • We give you a price quotation.
  • Upon receiving your approval and initial payment, we assign you to one of our highly skilled and experienced editors.
  • Your document is edited, and all changes are clearly indicated. Your editor will include comments to aid you in your future writing, and your editor will discuss the document and changes with you.
  • Final payment is due when you are satisfied with the edited document.
